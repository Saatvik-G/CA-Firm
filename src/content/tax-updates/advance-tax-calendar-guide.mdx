## Statutory Provisions

Under **Section 208 of the Income Tax Act, 1961**, every assessee whose estimated tax liability for the financial year (after reducing TDS/TCS) is **₹10,000 or more** is statutorily required to pay advance tax in specified installments.

---

## 1. Advance Tax Installment Schedule

For corporate and non-corporate assessees (excluding presumptive tax under Section 44AD/44ADA):

- **1st Installment (By June 15)**: Minimum **15%** of estimated net tax liability.
- **2nd Installment (By September 15)**: Minimum **45%** of estimated net tax liability.
- **3rd Installment (By December 15)**: Minimum **75%** of estimated net tax liability.
- **4th Installment (By March 15)**: **100%** of estimated net tax liability.

*Note: Assesses eligible under Section 44AD or 44ADA presumptive scheme can pay 100% of advance tax in a single installment on or before **March 15**.*

---

## 2. Interest Charges for Deferment & Default

### A. Section 234C (Deferment of Installments)
Interest at **1% per month** (simple interest) is levied for shortfall in individual quarterly installments:
- Shortfall in June installment (if paid is < 12%) -> 1% per month for 3 months.
- Shortfall in September installment (if paid is < 36%) -> 1% per month for 3 months.
- Shortfall in December installment (if paid is < 75%) -> 1% per month for 3 months.
- Shortfall in March installment (if paid is < 100%) -> 1% per month for 1 month.

### B. Section 234B (Default in Payment of Advance Tax)
If total advance tax paid before March 31 is less than **90%** of the assessed tax, simple interest at **1% per month** is charged from April 1 of the assessment year until the date of regular assessment or return filing.

---

## Recommendation for Assesses

Ensure quarterly cash flow reviews and income estimation adjustments ahead of each installment date, taking into account unexpected capital gains or dividend receipts.
