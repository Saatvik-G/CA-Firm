## Key Provisions & Compliance Alert

The Central Board of Indirect Taxes and Customs (CBIC) has tightened automated checks on the GSTN portal under **Rule 36(4) and Rule 88D** of the CGST Rules, 2017.

---

## 1. Automated DRC-01C System-Generated Notices

Where the Input Tax Credit (ITC) availed in **Form GSTR-3B** exceeds the ITC available in **Form GSTR-2B** by a specified threshold (both percentage and absolute amount limit), the portal automatically issues a system notification in **Form GST DRC-01C (Part A)**.

### Statutory Obligations upon receiving DRC-01C:
1. Pay the excess ITC availed along with interest under Section 50 using **Form DRC-03**, **OR**
2. Submit a detailed technical reply in **Form DRC-01C (Part B)** explaining reasons for the mismatch (e.g., transit inventory, previous period credit notes, or erroneous vendor filings).
3. Response must be filed within **7 days** of receipt of DRC-01C.

> [!WARNING]
> If no payment is made or explanation accepted within 7 days, the portal blocks the generation of **GSTR-1** for subsequent tax periods under Rule 59(6).

---

## 2. Best Practices for Monthly ITC Reconciliation

- Execute monthly purchase ledger vs. GSTR-2B reconciliation before finalizing GSTR-3B liability.
- Categorize unmatched entries into:
  - Vendors who failed to file GSTR-1 (Send automated commercial alerts).
  - Invoices appearing in GSTR-2B with incorrect POS or tax head (CGST/SGST vs IGST).
  - Ineligible ITC items under Section 17(5) (e.g., motor vehicles, personal consumption).
- Retain detailed reconciliation workpapers certified by your CA for statutory verification.
